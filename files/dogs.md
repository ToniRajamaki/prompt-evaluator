# Dogs

Dogs are domesticated mammals known for loyalty, social intelligence, and close relationships with humans. They vary widely in size, appearance, temperament, and working ability depending on breed and individual background.

## Key Traits

- Scientific name: *Canis lupus familiaris*
- Diet: Omnivorous, with carnivorous ancestry
- Average lifespan: 10–15 years
- Common behaviors: Barking, sniffing, fetching, guarding, herding, bonding

## Interesting Facts

Dogs have a highly developed sense of smell and can be trained for search and rescue, detection work, assistance tasks, therapy support, and livestock management.

## Care Notes

Dogs need daily exercise, social interaction, training, fresh water, a balanced diet, veterinary care, and mental stimulation. Their care requirements vary greatly by size, breed, age, and energy level.
