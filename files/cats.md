# Cats

Cats are small domesticated mammals known for their independence, agility, and strong hunting instincts. They are common companion animals and are valued for their affectionate behavior, quiet presence, and ability to adapt to many living environments.

## Key Traits

- Scientific name: *Felis catus*
- Diet: Carnivorous
- Average lifespan: 12–18 years
- Common behaviors: Grooming, climbing, scratching, purring, stalking

## Interesting Facts

Cats use their whiskers to sense nearby objects and judge narrow spaces. They also communicate through body posture, tail movement, vocal sounds, and scent marking.

## Care Notes

Cats need fresh water, a balanced diet, regular veterinary care, scratching surfaces, play, and safe resting places. Indoor cats benefit from enrichment such as climbing trees, puzzle feeders, and interactive toys.
