# Mice

Mice are small rodents known for quick movement, curiosity, adaptability, and high reproductive rates. They live in many environments and are important in ecosystems as both seed dispersers and prey animals.

## Key Traits

- Common genus: *Mus*
- Diet: Omnivorous; seeds, grains, fruit, insects, and plant material
- Average lifespan: 1–3 years, depending on species and conditions
- Common behaviors: Nesting, burrowing, grooming, exploring, gnawing

## Interesting Facts

Mice have sharp senses of smell and hearing. They use scent marking and high-pitched vocalizations to communicate with each other.

## Care Notes

Pet mice need a secure enclosure, bedding for burrowing, chew-safe toys, hiding places, clean water, balanced food, and careful handling. They are social animals but should be grouped appropriately to avoid aggression or unwanted breeding.
