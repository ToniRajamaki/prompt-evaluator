# Birds

Birds are feathered, egg-laying vertebrates found in nearly every habitat on Earth. They are known for flight, song, migration, complex social behavior, and diverse forms ranging from tiny hummingbirds to large flightless ostriches.

## Key Traits

- Class: *Aves*
- Diet: Varies by species; seeds, fruit, insects, nectar, fish, or small animals
- Common abilities: Flight, vocal learning, nest building, navigation
- Notable features: Feathers, beaks, hollow bones, eggshells

## Interesting Facts

Some birds migrate thousands of kilometers each year. Others, such as parrots and corvids, are known for problem-solving ability and advanced vocal communication.

## Care Notes

Pet birds need suitable cage space, social interaction, species-appropriate food, clean water, enrichment, safe perches, and regular veterinary care. Many species are highly social and require daily attention.
